<summary><strong>Original Interview Question</strong></summary>

**Q:** You're building a simple spam classifier for emails. What's the difference between precision and recall, and which metric would you prioritize if false positives are more costly than false negatives?

**A:** Precision = TP/(TP+FP) measures the accuracy of positive predictions. Recall = TP/(TP+FN) measures the ability to identify all actual positives. When false positives are more costly than false negatives, prioritize precision to minimize incorrectly classified legitimate emails as spam.
