> **Understanding How does Kubernetes handle pod scheduling during cluster autoscaling events, and what scheduling considerations come into play when new nodes are added or removed from the cluster?**

## Overview

During cluster autoscaling events, the Kubernetes scheduler collaborates with the cluster autoscaler to place pods on newly created nodes while accounting for initialization delays. When scaling down, the scheduler manages pod eviction and rescheduling as nodes are removed from the cluster.
