> **Understanding How do Kubernetes pod priority and preemption work together to ensure critical workloads get scheduled during resource contention, and what are the potential pitfalls of relying heavily on preemption?**

## Overview

Pod priority classes establish scheduling precedence, while preemption enables higher-priority pods to evict lower-priority ones to secure resources during resource contention. Excessive reliance on preemption can lead to increased pod churn and inefficient resource utilization.
