**Q:** What is LoRA and how does it reduce parameters when fine-tuning large language models?

**A:** LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation) adds low-rank matrices to frozen pretrained weights, reducing trainable parameters by training only small adapter matrices instead of the full model weights.
