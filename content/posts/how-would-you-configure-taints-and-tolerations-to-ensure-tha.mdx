> **Understanding How would you configure taints and tolerations to ensure that GPU workloads are scheduled exclusively on GPU-equipped nodes while allowing critical monitoring pods to tolerate these taints, and what happens when no GPU nodes are available?**

## Overview

Configure node taints with effects like NoSchedule or PreferNoSchedule, combined with pod tolerations that include matching key-value pairs and optional tolerationSeconds. When no GPU nodes are available, pods with matching tolerations remain in a Pending state until a GPU node becomes available or the scheduling criteria are met.
