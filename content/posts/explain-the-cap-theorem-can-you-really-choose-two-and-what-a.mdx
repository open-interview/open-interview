> **Understanding Explain the CAP Theorem. Can you really 'choose two' and what are the practical tradeoffs?**

## Overview

The CAP Theorem states that distributed systems can only guarantee two out of three properties: Consistency, Availability, and Partition Tolerance. In practice, since network partitions are inevitable, the real tradeoff is between Consistency-Partition (CP) and Availability-Partition (AP) systems.
